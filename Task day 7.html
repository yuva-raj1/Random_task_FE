Task 7: Recipe List

    Lets cook upma

  • Ingredients
    • Upma flour
    • Onions
    • Coriander leavess
    • Red Chillies : 4-5
    • Water
    • Oil
    • Salt 3tbspn
  • Steps to follow
    1. Take a bowl and heat upma flour for 5min in a low flame
    2. Take a bowl,put on 2tbspn of oil in it, let it heat for 2min
    3. After oil gets heated, add onions to it, let it boil for a 5min in low flame.
    4. Then add coriander leaves to it.
    5. Then add Red chillies to it and let close the lid for 2-3min
    6. Then pour water in a ratio 2:1 as compared to upma flour and add salt to it.
    7. Stir the recipe and add flour to it slowly and make sure to stir the recipe effectively.
    8. Finally upma recipe is ready
